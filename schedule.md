# Tentative Lecture Schedule

{:.no_toc}

- This schedule is only tentaive and may change drastically during the quarter. The exam date, however, will not change.
- Slides will usually be posted on the course [home page]({{ '/' | relative_url }}) before each lecture. Since lectures may differ substantially from the textbook, attending live or watching recordings promptly is the best way to stay on track.
- Guest lectures are **not part of the exam syllabus**, but they will be central to the extra credit activities.
